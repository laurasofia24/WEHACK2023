GAME-OF-THE-MONTH: MR. CURSOR

BREAKING NEWS: Mr. Cursor became the most-played game on EasyPeasyProg within one month of its release!

Mr. Cursor is a game that tests your error-detection skills. Error-detection is an important skill to have in general, but especially when addressing flaws in your code, also known as debugging. The game gives you a block of code, and you select which line in that block has flawed code. Meanwhile, Mr. Cursor navigates to the "Run" button. Ideally, your program would be error-free before you run it. Therefore, the objective is to detect all of the errors before he reaches the "Run" button. The questions present different kinds of syntax errors so that they can be easily avoided when you begin coding larger-scale projects independently. This game keeps you on your feet and has many real-life applications! It is one of the most highly-rated games on this platform (rightfully so).
